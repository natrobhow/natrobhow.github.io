Research

Heidi Craig's research divides into three main areas: Early Modern Literature & Culture; Rag Collection & Textual Culture; and Digital Humanities & Bibliography.



I am the author of Theatre Closure and the Paradoxical Rise of English Renaissance Drama in the Civil Wars (Cambridge University Press, 2023).

which examines the lasting impact of the theatrical prohibition of 1642 to 1660 on English dramatic culture. This monograph argues that the death of live theatre in the 1640s and 1650s led to the birth of English Renaissance Drama as a genre and field. IMAGETEST1

I have published on the centrality of rag pickers to textual and literary culture, and has begun her second monograph, Working with Waste: Writing and Scavenging from Rags to E-Waste on the interdependence of waste workers and writers from the early modern period to the present.

I am the editor of two large-scale digital databases, the World Shakespeare Bibliography, and Early Modern Dramatic Paratexts, which I co-created and co-edit with Sonia Massai.
