Assistant Professor, CLTA, University of Toronto
heidi.craig@utoronto.ca

She is the author of Theatre Closure and the Paradoxical Rise of English Renaissance Drama in the Civil Wars (Cambridge University Press, 2023), and has published numerous articles and chapters on digital bibliography and pedagogy, Shakespeare and early modern literature, and textual culture.

She has served as editor for two large-scale digital resources: World Shakespeare Bibliography and Early Modern Dramatic Paratexts the latter co-created with Sonia Massai.

Her next book project examines the interdependence of rag pickers and writers from the early modern period to the present.

Heidi Craig completed her PhD at the University of Toronto in 2017. Prior to returning to the University of Toronto, she was an Assistant Professor of English at Texas A&M University from 2019 to 2023.
